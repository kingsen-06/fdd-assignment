    General Information

    What is this hackathon about? +

    This hackathon is an innovation-focused event where participants collaborate to build technology solutions.

    The hackathon is open to students and beginners interested in technology, programming, and design.

    No prior experience is required. Mentors will be available throughout the event.

    You may participate individually or as a team. Team matching will be available.

    The hackathon will be held on 15/1/2026 at APU campus.
